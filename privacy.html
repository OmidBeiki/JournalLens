Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 November 2025

JournalLens is a lightweight reference app that helps you assess the credibility of academic journals using open, public data. We designed this app to collect as little personal information as possible.

1) Who we are

Controller: JournalLens (published personally by Omid Beiki, Sweden).

Contact: journallens.app@gmail.com

2) What the app does

JournalLens queries public registries/sources to display journal metadata and integrity signals:

  • OpenAlex (journal/source metadata)
  • Crossref (publisher/journal endpoints)
  • DOAJ (open-access index)
  • COPE (committee membership)

3) Personal data we collect

We do not require an account and do not collect personally identifiable information in the app.

  • Local device data: recent searches may be stored locally on your device to make repeat lookups faster. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by reinstalling the app.
  • Network requests: when the app calls public APIs listed above, standard technical logs (e.g., IP address, user-agent) may be recorded by those services or by your network provider. JournalLens does not build profiles or track users.
  • Support contact: if you email us, we receive your email address and message contents solely to handle the request.

4) Cookies & analytics

We do not use cookies or third-party analytics inside the app.

5) Children’s privacy

JournalLens is intended for general audiences in research/education and does not target children. No sensitive content is presented.

6) Legal basis (EU/EEA)

  • Legitimate interests: operating a journal-lookup tool and responding to support requests.
  • Consent: not required because we do not track or profile users; if this changes, we will request consent.

7) Data sharing & processors

We do not sell personal data. The app communicates with external public APIs (OpenAlex, Crossref, DOAJ, COPE). Their servers may process your IP address to deliver responses. Please consult their respective privacy statements for details.

8) Data retention

  • On device: recent searches remain until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Support emails: retained only as long as necessary to resolve your request.

9) Your rights

If you contact us, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your support correspondence. Because the app does not maintain user accounts or server-side profiles, there is no additional personal data to access or erase.

10) International transfers

The public APIs we call are hosted in various regions. Your network requests may be routed internationally according to standard internet operations.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (for example, if future versions add accounts, notifications, or language options). Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
