Quick start

  1. Open the app and choose Search by Title or Search by ISSN.
  2. Start typing; the app suggests likely matches from OpenAlex.
  3. Select a journal to view publisher details, DOAJ/COPE status, and a color-coded credibility indicator.

FAQ

Which sources does JournalLens use?

OpenAlex, Crossref, DOAJ, and COPE. Links to source entries are shown when available.

Does the app store my personal data?

No. The current version does not use accounts. Recent searches may be stored locally on your device to speed up repeat lookups.

Why does a journal show “Amber” or “Red”?

The indicator reflects signals from sources (e.g., missing DOAJ entry, unclear affiliations) and basic ISSN checks. It is guidance only—always review primary sources.

How can I report incorrect data?

Email us with the journal title/ISSN and screenshots or links. We will verify against primary sources and improve matching rules where possible.

Will there be notifications or more features?

Possibly in future versions (e.g., alerts for retractions or status changes). If we add these, we will update the Privacy Policy and request permissions when required.
